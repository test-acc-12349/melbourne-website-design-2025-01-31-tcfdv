Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build a website?

Most projects are completed within 2-4 weeks. Simple landing pages can be done in as little as 1 week, while more complex e-commerce sites might take 6-8 weeks.

What's included in your hosting package?

Our enterprise hosting package includes daily backups, SSL certificates, 99.9% uptime guarantee, CDN integration, and 24/7 security monitoring - all at no additional cost.

Do you offer ongoing support and maintenance?

Yes, we provide comprehensive support packages starting at $99/month, including regular updates, security patches, content updates, and technical support.

What is your payment structure?

We require a 50% deposit to begin work, with the remaining balance due upon project completion. We also offer flexible payment plans for larger projects.

Do you help with SEO and marketing?

Yes, all our websites are built with SEO best practices in mind. We also offer additional digital marketing packages to help drive traffic and conversions.
